WHAT WE DO?

Experience the Best Care at africohse

AFRICOHSE is a comprehensive

institution dedicated to protecting,

promoting and maintaining the health

and wellbeing of the workforce in

Kenya.

The Centre operates by fulfilling three

distinct yet interconnected functions:

SERVICE PROVISION, CORPORATE

WELLNESS, AND EDUCATION AND

TRAINING in the fields of occupational

medicine and occupational health.

These services cater to both public

and private sectors, ensuring

comprehensive support for the health

and well-being of all individuals in the

workforce.

Our Specializations

 We have assembled a diverse team of experts

dedicated to delivering high-quality services.

Our team of occupational health practitioners

includes: consultant physicians, occupational

health nurses, occupational therapists,

hygienists, physiotherapists, counselors,

psychologists, epidemiologists, technicians,

registered nurses, and physiologists.

Africohse has a team of experienced medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, physician assistants, and medical assistants.

Africohse offers a wide range of diagnostic testing, including blood tests, X-rays, MRIs, and ultrasounds.
